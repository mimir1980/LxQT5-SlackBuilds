LxQt Built against Qt5 for Slackware Linux Current

libxcb is too old in 14.1 (Or maybe it just hates me)

On Slackware 14.1 upgrade the libxcb package before compiling Qt5

If you use a session manager to log in, if you change a system theme or your settings and you log out and then
back in, openbox may not start and you won't have a window manager and have to reboot. I log in through the linux console
using a xinitrc and don't have this problem.
